Investing for Income

If you’re a new investor and visiting Income Investing for the first time, then this page is what you need to get started.

When I first started investing, the Internet was still in its infancy. The resources available to increase my investment knowledge were very limited compared to what’s available today.

I didn’t have wealthy friends or family to guide me, so my only option was to read as many personal finance books and magazines I could get my hands on, and experiment with my own finances. After trial and error from my own experience, I made it one of my goals to help educate others about finance.
